Introduction:

Mountain View origins as a stagecoach stop, it grew to a large suburb with a pedestrian-friendly downtown, free wi-fi, and a population of 74,066. The city borders Palo Alto and the San Francisco Bay to the north, Los Altos to the south, and Moffett Federal Airfield and Sunnyvale to the east.Situated in the southern end of the San Francisco Peninsula in the San Francisco Bay Area, Mountain View is home to many high technology companies.

Local Bookstore:

This bookstore is one of the very best places to visit in town. Within the bookstore, you can find not only best-selling novels, and also many interesting things, including cute postcards, colorful notebooks, little toys, and even name-brand water bottles. The environment is friendly, quiet, and full of surprise. We could spend 20 minutes there just for glancing around, or you can spend an hour to deep in what they have. I would like to bring out my favorite part of the store, which is a postcard with such wonderful quote by Bill Cliton:"Be brave and chase your dream".
